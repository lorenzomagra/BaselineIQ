    Project 4 — done

    Match analysis

    Our Team vs AS Rivéry (sample match) — quarter-by-quarter breakdown to identify the runs that decided the game.

    Cumulative score progression by quarter

    Key moments of the match

    Q1-Q2 — Even game

    38-35 at halftime, tight pace, neither team leads by more than 5 points.

    Q3 — 12-2 opponent run

    AS Rivéry tightens its defense and strings together transition baskets; we enter the final quarter trailing 53-57.

    Q4 — Collective response

    14-2 run early in the period after a defensive timeout; the game swings for good, final score 78-71.

    Flash report conclusion

    The timeout called at the start of the 4th quarter was the turning point — the defensive adjustment (low-post help) cut the opponent's rhythm and relaunched our offensive transition. A point worth keeping for the next game prep.

    Project 5 — done

    Opponent scouting report

    One-page prep sheet — example on a fictional opponent, format directly reusable club by club.

    Scouting sheet

    ES Argentan — next opponent

    Offensive tendencies

    • 68% of possessions go through the low post (their #5, physically dominant)
    • Low 3-point % as a team (28% on the season)
    • Fast transition game off every defensive rebound secured

    Key player

    Point guard #4 — 17 pts/game, primary outside catch-and-shoot threat, main danger late in the shot clock.

    Suggested adjustments

    • Double-team the low post as soon as the ball is received
    • Pressure their outlet pass in transition — frequent turnovers under pressure
    • Never let point guard #4 shoot catch-and-shoot — contest every outside shot
