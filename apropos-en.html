About

Where the court meets the data

Stats and video analysis remain largely reserved for elite-level basketball today, due to a lack of accessible tools for amateur clubs and youth categories. BaselineIQ exists to fill that gap.

Trained in coaching (BPJEPS Basketball), I'm building data and video expertise in parallel to deliver performance analysis that's directly usable in weekly training: player profiles, team dashboards, opponent scouting and video reports.

“I'm not a data analyst who picked up basketball, nor a coach tinkering with Excel sheets. Both skill sets are built together, to the same standard.”

Every project in this portfolio was built independently, ahead of any formal Data Analyst training. Proof by example rather than by diploma.
